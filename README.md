## General Information
This is done part of Exploratory Data Analysis case study
The company is a consumer finance company which specialises in lending various types of loans to urban customers. When the company receives a loan application, the company has to make a decision for loan approval based on the applicantâ€™s profile. 

- Business probem
  - The company wants to understand the driving factors (or driver variables) behind loan default, i.e. the variables which are strong indicators of default.  The company can utilise this knowledge for its portfolio and risk assessment. 

- Dataset being used
  - We used loan.csv file for this analysis
